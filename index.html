Brew Your Coffee

Brew time: 2:30 - 3:30 minutes

Coffee dose: 20 - 27 grams

Grind size: medium

Water: 350 grams

How To Do Pour-over

  • 1. Heat the water

    Heat water to 92–96°C (195–205°F). Just below boiling is ideal.

  • 2. Grind the coffee

    Use a medium-fine grind (similar to sea salt). Ratio: 1:15 to 1:17. Example: 20g coffee : 300ml water.

  • 3.Rinse the filter

    Place the filter in the dripper. Rinse with hot water to remove paper taste and preheat. Discard rinse water.

  • 4.Add coffee grounds

    Add ground coffee to the filter. Gently shake to level the bed.

  • 5.Bloom (30–45 seconds)

    Pour 2–3 times the coffee weight in water (e.g., 40–60ml for 20g coffee). Let it sit to release trapped CO₂. This improves extraction.

  • 6. Main pour

    Slowly pour the remaining water in circular motions. Start from the center, spiral outward, avoid pouring directly on the filter. Keep a steady, controlled flow.

  • 7.Drawdown and serve

    Total brew time: 2:30 - 3:30 minutes. Remove dripper and enjoy.
